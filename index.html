Foosball routine

Put the ball into play on your five bar like you would for a normal match

Click the "Start training" button

As soon as you hear GO start moving the ball on your 5 bar

Follow instructions!

Passes: "wall pass", "lane pass", "center pass" (to your middle man on the three bar)

Shots: "long left", "short left", "straight", "short right", "long right", "cut back left", "cut back right" (angle shot)
